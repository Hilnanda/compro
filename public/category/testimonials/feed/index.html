How John Clements Became a Lifesaver for JFE Techno Manila


Established in 1995 as NKK Techno Manila and renamed in 2003, JFE Techno Manila, Inc. is a subsidiary of JFE Engineering Corporation, one of the leading engineering companies in Japan.  Some of their main tasks are providing engineering design and support services for various business fields, and to carry out their plans and steer their company forward, the company elected John Clements Consultants, Inc. as the best source for hiring pioneers and talents.

“We hire many people through John Clements.  Most of them now have good careers.  By ourselves, usually we hire fresh graduates, but it’s very hard to hire good career persons.  So we need John Clements for that.  They have been a big help to us in hiring for experienced or manager-level positions.”

Yamagishi is proud to say that they have been working hand-in-hand with John Clements for 20 years, trusting in the company’s impressive track record and excellent service.  He adds that it is this same commitment to quality that sets John Clements apart and makes it a market leader for recruitment and HR solutions in the Philippines.

“With John Clements’ very long experience in the industry, they are well-known to jobseekers, particularly those looking for career growth,” he said.  “They are [distinguished] in the Philippines and have group companies such as EDI Staffbuilders, who have a database for OFWs who opt to settle in the Philippines.”

In addition to utilizing John Clements’ recruiting services, Yamagishi has a special place in his heart for EDI Staffbuilders, sharing that, “[Besides getting recruitment help from] EDI Staffbuilders, a subsidiary of John Clements, the president of EDI, Mr. Averia, is my friend and is one of our directors.  When we established JFE, then under its old name NKK Techno Manila, he helped us establish and grow the company.”

Truly, when you work with John Clements you gain a partner for life – someone who is more than just a consultant and advisor, but also a support system and friend.

How John Clements Eased Frontier Software’s Recruitment and Hiring

Frontier Software is a world leader in providing HR and Payroll Management software.  It has served more than 1,500 organizations in both public and private sectors all over the world, and is currently on its 4th year in the Philippines, bringing its revolutionary products and services to the country’s corporate landscape.

Back in 2013 when Frontier Software opened their new office in Manila, one of their main concerns was hiring the best team members to spearhead their plans, manage their offices, and carry out their mission in the local field.  But as founder and managing director Michael Howard stated, when it came to hiring, there was just one corporation for the job: John Clements Consultants, Inc.

“[Frontier Software has been a client of John Clements] since I started the company here in the Philippines,” he shares.  “We started with quality assurance; I gave them job specifications, and then they matched these specifications, came up with candidates and […] found a number of applicants suitable for positions.”

Frontier Software continues to rely on John Clements for all its recruitment and development needs, leveraging on their years of experience and expertise.  Howard acknowledges John Clements as a trusted partner, and is a firm advocate of their success.

“[The benefit Frontier Software had] from working with John Clements was that we didn’t have to do the work,” Howard recalls.

 “They found the applicants, and we were very pleased with the results.  The applicants were of very high standard, and they actually met our specifications, from a testing point of view, which is quality assurance, from a software development point of view, and from a telemarketer point of view.  John Clements even helped us fill the executive position for Finance Manager, which was more of a senior management role.”

With the trust that Howard has placed in John Clements Consultants, Inc. and their track record of keeping that trust, he knows he is working with the best.  Frontier Software has relied on John Clements for its entire stay in the Philippines, and will continue to do so thanks to the company’s consistently good placements and development results.

“The final question is: have they searched and have they found good applicants?  And the answer is yes,” he says proudly.

How Rose Katalbas Experienced John Clements’ End-to-End Commitment to Service

As both a candidate and client of John Clements Consultants, Inc., SunPower Corporation’s Vice President for Human Resources Rose Katalbas has experienced exceptional and loyal service from both ends of the corporation.

“I have been a client of John Clements for many years,” she shares.  “I met Mario [Biscocho, Executive Search and Selection Division Head] when I was starting in my career, and we have worked together for all this time.  John Clements is a legend in the industry.”

Having experienced working with John Clements for so many years, Katalbas has developed and maintained a strong relationship with the company.  She stated, “[My] relationship with John Clements has been both professional and personal.  They’ve helped me place critical positions, those that are very difficult to search in the Philippines.  John Clements has a network in Asia, which is why we have also tapped them for that.  They’ve helped me source, especially for very difficult positions that may or may not be available in the Philippines.”

“John Clements provides insights [about] what’s going on in the market, and what’s going on in the Philippines at large.  They really don’t have to do that, but they do.  So I think that John Clements is not just a search firm, it’s a business enterprise that caters to the needs of every customer.”

Katalbas is a staunch advocate of John Clements’ services and relationship management because she’s been a grateful recipient herself.  She knows that until today, she has a loyal support system that she can rely on.

“I would make a call and always get entertained.  I would ask for advice, or meet up and try to do a self-assessment, and they would give insights,” she recalled.  “Besides being very professional people to deal with, John Clements is strong in its adjacent services, like providing newsletters, business trends, and Harvard Business Review articles.  John Clements is also strong in relationship management with the client, and extremely flexible.  It is not easy to deal with an industry that is very changeable, but John Clements is very patient to understand that.  They also try to connect clients [with each other].  They would host activities, invite us to expert talks.  They’re very well-rounded, and are even growing beyond the country.”

Years of Commitment and Service: Tupperware’s Story with John Clements

Introduced in the Philippines back in 1966, Tupperware Brands Philippines has since become a household name.  Having merged with Sara Lee Direct Selling Philippines in 2007, it is now recognized as a “multi-brand, multi-category direct sales company” that develops an independent door-to-door sales force as well as recruits individuals who sell Tupperware’s products themselves.

For majority of its time in the country, Tupperware Brands has relied on John Clements for its recruitment and consulting needs.  HR Director Jane Montilla is a staunch believer in the John Clements brand, having been a client for such a long time herself.

“I’ve been a client of John Clements for more than twenty years,” Montilla shares.  “To me personally, it has been a very reliable partner, and because our relationship has been so long, they’re actually like family already to me and my husband, who also had a lot of experiences [with them] especially during Cady [Dominguez, John Clements founder’s] time.  I feel secure having them as my partner, because I know I can always run to them and they will always be there to assist me.”

With its unbeaten commitment to service and years of experience in the industry, John Clements surely has much to be proud of.  As an industry gamechanger in its own right, the company has been pioneering services and solutions in the Philippines for all of its 40 years.

“John Clements is the largest HR consulting firm in the Philippines, and I would like to think they’re also probably the oldest,” Montilla says. “So it has stability, in addition to the depth of their expertise, and also their network is much greater than any other consulting firm in the Philippines.

“Those are just a few benefits of working with John Clements: their wide and deep experience in the area of HR consulting, their network, their stability as a company, and like I said, to me, it’s always having a reliable partner with you that will always be there and won’t leave you hanging every time you need help.”

Montilla has not only enjoyed the benefits of these services; she’s also watched the company grow alongside her own, and is quick to express her faith and pride in how far John Clements has come.

“I always recommend others to use John Clements, whether it’s Executive Search, Staffing, Training,” she says.  “Especially when Carol [Dominguez, John Clements’ President and CEO] came in; she turned it around and made it very competitive, very fresh.  I know that she also has a program of training everybody in the company so that they are updated and they keep their competency levels better than par, because they’re expected to be consultants to the rest of the business community.  I like the way John Clements has evolved and grown and made itself more relevant, especially through tie-ups with Harvard, that sort of thing.  It’s actually very refreshing.”
