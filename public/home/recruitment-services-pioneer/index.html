OUR HERITAGE

1974

Mr. John Clements, Australian psychologist, and Mr. Cady Dominguez, Harvard Business School graduate, sealed the partnership of John Clements Consultants, Inc. for Executive Search and set its sights on becoming a pioneer in the recruitment business.

1976

Looking at the growing need for staffing of professionals, middle management down to rank and file, Professional Staffers Division was born in 1976.

1979

In 1979, Mr. Dominguez formed EDI Staffbuilders with Mr. John Corrigan, an Australian investment banker, to initially service projects in Saudi Arabia.

1984

Saudi Arabia was suffering from a recession. Foreign investors were jittery and pulling out. This included John Corrigan, the partner of John Clements Consultants, Inc. in EDI Staffbuilders.

1991

In 1991, another promising market caught Mr. Dominguez’s eyes: Jakarta. He sent two of his consultants to case the joint, so to speak, and to set up a manual of future operations.

1995

Two more divisions formed in 1995 – Speednet and Shop ‘n Check Philippines

2002

In August 2002, Mr. Dominguez – without fuss or fanfare – suddenly fell ill and said goodbye to John Clements Consultants, Inc., the company he gave life to, nurtured, and grew for 28 years.

2004

In 2004, new developments emerged in the corporate world.  Offshore Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) was huge and rapidly growing at an unprecedented rate. The call center industry was the fastest growing industry in world, and because of that we pitched the capability of the Philippines to handle BPO/IT and shared services.

2005

John Clements’ Vision Mission Values were forged.

March 2, 2005

Speaking on John Clements’ behalf, Magellan Alliance, a U.S. consulting firm, presented to an SRO audience at the American Chamber of Commerce, initial results of a study on the overall potential of the Philippines as a major BPO destination.

April 2005

Launched the maiden issue of Wazzup, John Clements’ official electronic corporate newsletter.

April 2005

John Clements introduced its English Foundation through its Work Force Development Program to help current and future employees communicate more effectively in English

April 2005

EDI-Staffbuilders International was cited as the benchmark in good recruitment practices by the International Labor Organization (ILO)

May 3, 2005

John Clements, through Carol Dominguez and Rocky Peltzman, vied for English as medium of education in the Batasan Basic Education and Culture sub-committee’s deliberation on the bill.

May 2005

JC’s top management identified Targeted Selection as new tool to improve hiring and promotion process

May 2005

In a call for good leaders in the BPO industry, JC took the initiative of offering its Leadership Training Seminars

June 2005

July 2005

JOHN CLEMENTS CONSULTANTS, INC.

Since its inception in 1974, John Clements Consultants, Inc. has grown into one of the largest consulting firms in the Philippines today, with a leadership position in the local market and a strengthening presence in the international scene, notably in Asia, the Middle East, and the US. While it has grown in size and business interests, locally and internationally, and prospects of further expansion remain in the horizon, the John Clements Consultants Group has maintained its focus on its core competencies: recruitment and training.
